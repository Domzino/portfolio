    Goop
    Short animated sequence
    Goop is a rough 3-minute animation, introducing the plot for a forthcoming sequel. It takes place on an alternative version of Earth, where everything is powered by a new source of fuel: goop.

    This animation was made in the first semester of my second year for the 'Anymation' module; it was a solo project which took a couple of months to create.

    Steampunk Tavern
    Low-poly steampunk tavern
    This Steampunk tavern was made for my low-poly 3D modelling module in second year. The whole map is made to be very efficient, with a single 512x512 png UVW map shared between every object.

    Feel free to try it below: Use arrow keys to walk, shift to run, space bar to jump

    Italy
    UDK map based loosely off cs_italy
    This is a piece I did for a 3D modelling module in my second year. It was based off the Counter-Strike map 'cs_italy', and was created using a mixture of photographs I took on my holiday to Spain and copyright free images.
    Attack On Atlantis
    Steampunk rail-shooter
    Attack on Atlantis is an underwater rail-shooter where the player is tasked with destroying the great city of Atlantis. It has a Steampunk theme and features cartoony aesthetics. The game was made during my 3rd year at university, where I was the lead artist and created all of the art assets (excluding the whale and the explosions, which were sourced online).

    Feel free to try the game below: Use the mouse to move your ship and hold/tap Left Mouse to shoot
    Kojo's Adventure
    Story-driven mini-game collection
    Kojo's Adventure is a story-driven minigame collection aimed at young children. It was a commission from the Ipswich Museum, and is themed around their 'Unlocked Collection'. All of the art you see is done by me, whilst the coding was done by Sean Edwick.

    Feel free to click the game below to try: Arrow keys = Joystick, 'Q' = Green, 'W' = Blue, 'E' = Red.
