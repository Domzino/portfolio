    • Dominic Noble
    2D Arist : 3D Artist : Animator

    My name is Dominic Noble, and I'm a 3D/2D artist living in Ipswich, UK. I have a 1st class degree in Computer Games Design and have been involved in many projects over the last few years.

    When I was 6 years old I received my first ever gaming console, the Nintendo 64. By the time I was 13, I was already attempting to make my own creative visions come to life with Macromedia Flash.

    As well as my versatile skillsets, I also consider myself to be a keen and knowledgeable game designer, leading the production of multiple games throughout my three year degree.
